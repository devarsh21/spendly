Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account (such as your name and email address) and the expense data you enter into the app (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions). We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power your personal expense tracking experience — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the core features of Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
